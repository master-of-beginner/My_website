Shanghai University of Finance and Economics

  1. Brief description

    Shanghai University of Finance and Economics (SUFE) was founded in 1917. After over 100 years’ development, this university has become one of the toppest business universities in China, and it is also ranked 3rd in mainland China and 9th in Asia in terms of Economics and Finance by Tilburg University’s Economics Schools Research Ranking.
