Those are my top 3 favorite food!

  1. Hotpot

    Hotpot also known as soup-food or steamboat, is a cooking method that originates from China, prepared with a simmering pot of soup stock at the dining table, containing a variety of Chinese foodstuffs and ingredients. It could have so many different tastes, including spicy, tomato flavor, mushroom flavor, etc.

  1. Hot and Spicy crayfish

    Crayfish is a seasonal dish in China. People cram into Crayfish restaurants and order by the bucket load. It would be a liitle complex to cook this meal, and different restaurants can server totally different crayfish dishes. Some are very delicious.

  1. Barbecue Barbecue is an extremely popular food in China for midnight snack. Friends usually meet up together to a barbecue restaurants with wines and beer. You can find barbecue restaurants almost in every Chinese City.
